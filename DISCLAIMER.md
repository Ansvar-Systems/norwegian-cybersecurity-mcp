## Data Authority and Limitations

### Source Authority

| Source | Authority Level | Verify At |
|--------|----------------|-----------|
| **NSM (Nasjonal sikkerhetsmyndighet)** | Authoritative for national security guidance | [nsm.no](https://nsm.no) |
| **NorCERT** | Authoritative for security advisories | [nsm.no/norcert](https://nsm.no/fagomrader/operativt-samarbeid/norcert/) |
| **Lovdata** | Authoritative for Norwegian legislation | [lovdata.no](https://lovdata.no) |

**This Tool contains summaries, not official guidance text.** The official text published on nsm.no and lovdata.no is the authoritative source.

## Currency and Staleness

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by weeks or months
- Norwegian cybersecurity guidance changes through new NSM publications, updated Grunnprinsipper, and new NorCERT advisories
- A document marked "current" may have been updated or superseded since the last database update
- This Tool does NOT automatically track updates to NSM guidance

## Coverage Gaps

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Full guidance text** -- contains representative summaries only
2. **Classified material** -- NSM classified guidance (UGRADERT/BEGRENSET/KONFIDENSIELT/HEMMELIG) is not included
3. **EU cybersecurity directives** -- NIS2 Directive, Cyber Resilience Act, etc. are covered separately by the EU Regulations MCP
4. **Sector-specific guidance** -- detailed sector regulations (finance, health, energy) are covered by dedicated MCPs
5. **Municipality-level security policies** -- local government security publications are not covered
6. **Real-time threat intelligence** -- current threat data from NSM/NorCERT feeds is not included
